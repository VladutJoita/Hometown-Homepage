Visit Brasov

Top three places to see in Brasov

Experience adventure parks and zip-lines

Parc Aventura is a great place if you want to spend some time in nature.

Visit the planetarium and the zoo garden

The planetarium and the zoo garden are the perfect choice to see some animals and learn something about other planets.

Have fun at Dash with games and activities

Play bowling, pool, ping-pong and other games.

Top three locations to visit in Brasov

Black Church

Black Church was build by the local Transylvanian Saxon(German) community of the city during medieval times and represents the main Gothic-style monument in the country.

Council Square

Council Square is located in the center of the old town and is one of the most visited places from Brasov.

Brasov Citadel

Brasov Citadel was build in 1580 and is one of the oldest buildings from Brasov.
